The SAP Learning Solution (LSO) offers an all-round training and education solution to satisfy customer's demands for a complete learning solution. The SAP Learning Solution has made wide-ranging, consistent enhancements to the classical Training and Event Management function, for example, by including e-learning as an element of a modern training Catalogue. SAP worked on developing an all-round solution right from the start.
